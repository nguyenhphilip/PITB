**Oranges**

<div class = "poem">
* What's real
* is not
* ideal.
* &nbsp;
* Peel
* an orange
* & feel
* &nbsp;
* its parts,
* &nbsp;
* how easily
* the whole
* breaks
* &nbsp;
* apart
* & still
* retains
* &nbsp;
* its tart,
* &nbsp;
* perfection
* an orange
* in every bite.
</div>
