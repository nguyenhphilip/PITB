---
title: "Our Dream"
date: "05-08-2020"
categories: [poems]
title-block-banner: false
---

<div class = "poem">
* The rainbow is our dream
* our sky sketched from creation,
* our soul canvas colored by
* dancing constellations.
* A place open to all
* (if you're not
* afraid of heights)
* A home for two who
* craft dreams
* by candlelight.
</div>
