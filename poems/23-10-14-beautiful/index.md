### Beautiful

::: poem
- Oh
- did you see that?
- It's so
- beautiful! 
- &nbsp;
- _And_
- _what about this?_
- &nbsp;
- Yes it's so,
- well I can't find
- the words for it
- but yes 
- it's so 
- beautiful!
- 
- _Wait a little_
- _longer._
- &nbsp;
- What do you mean?
- It's so beautiful!
- 
- _We might discover_
- _something more_
- _if we hold_
- _our tongues._
- &nbsp;
- Mmm, 
- like this?
- 
- _Yes,_
- _like this._
:::
