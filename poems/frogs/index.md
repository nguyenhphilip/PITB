title: "Frogs"
date: "06-20-2020"
categories: [poems]
title-block-banner: false
---

<div class = "poem">
* Find me 
* on the forest floor 
* lingering
* with the song
* of frogs
* leaping 
* from one stone
* to the next
* preaching words
* of wonder
* under shadows
* and
* sunlit beams
* of self-forgetting.
</div>
